Freight 101: Basics for Beginners

Welcome to the Freight 101 module! Here we cover essential terms and concepts to get started in the freight and logistics industry.

  • Carrier: The company or individual transporting the freight.
  • Shipper: The company or person sending the goods.
  • Load: The freight or cargo to be transported.
  • Bill of Lading: Document detailing the shipment.
  • Tracking: Monitoring freight location and status.
