Freight 101: Basics for Beginners

Welcome to the Freight 101 module! Here we cover the essential terms and concepts to get you started in the freight and logistics industry.

What is Freight? Freight is the transportation of goods by truck, ship, train, or aircraft.

Key Concepts:

  • Carrier: The company or individual transporting the freight.
  • Shipper: The company or person sending the goods.
  • Load: The freight or cargo to be transported.
  • Bill of Lading: A document detailing the shipment.
  • Tracking: Monitoring the freight location and status.
