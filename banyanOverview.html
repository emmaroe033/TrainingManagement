Banyan Overview

What is Banyan Technology?

Banyan Technology provides software solutions for freight shipping, primarily a Transportation Management System (TMS) that connects shippers, brokers, and 3PLs to a large network of carriers.

How Banyan Works

  • Banyan integrates with many carriers via APIs and EDI.
  • Customers use Banyan to compare carrier rates, book shipments, track deliveries, and manage freight invoices.
  • Banyan simplifies shipping by acting as a single platform connecting clients to many carriers.

Who Uses Banyan?

  • Shippers: Companies that need to transport goods, such as manufacturers and retailers.
  • 3PLs (Third-Party Logistics): Companies managing shipping for others.
  • Freight Brokers: Intermediaries connecting shippers with carriers.
  • Carriers: Trucking and freight companies connected to Banyan to receive load opportunities.

Why Clients Choose Banyan

  • Consolidated platform for managing multiple carriers and contracts.
  • Automated rate comparison and booking.
  • Real-time shipment tracking.
  • Invoice auditing and payment management.
  • Data and analytics to optimize shipping operations.
